Brownie Recipe



Description

This brownie recipe makes rich, fudgy brownies that always turn out well. They're topped with homemade chocolate frosting to make the best brownies ever!

Quick to make and delicious, this recipe is perfect for any occasion.


Ingredients

For the brownies
  • Butter
  • White Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Cocoa Powder
  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Salt
  • Baking Powder
For the frosting
  • Butter
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Honey
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Confectioners' Sugar
